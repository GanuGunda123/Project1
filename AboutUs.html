About Us

Discover the story behind Kiara Jewellery

KIARA

A specialist in gold and platinum diamond-studded jewellery since 15 years

Kiara is based on Seepz in Mumbai and has a skilled team that can grant a large capacity of production of over 10,000 pieces a month. Best practices evolved from Lean and Kaizen are fully implemented and ongoing.

Manufacturing processes are supported by best machines and cellular way of manufacturing; well-known ERP software with end-to-end are implemented. They care about top quality of our jewels with advanced electrochemical surface polishing, micro-setting for fine pave work, laser for welding and stamping and so on. Over the last years, they built an exclusive collection with different concepts for the International Market.

One Stop / One Shop Unit – In house experts from designing till finished products.

AURORA

A large offer of jewellery products

Aurora is an Italian company that offers you a large range of jewellery products. Aurora Srl is a purchasing, distribution and logistics platform based in Italy with 20 years of experience. It possesses a huge portfolio of suppliers of primers and finished products. Its dynamic team is always on the lookout for the newest trends and innovation to propose the best products on the market. The Italian office is also an important link for European customers to get access to the group’s production sites abroad.

SMS

40 years of jewellery production on the paradise island of Mauritius

From its French roots in Lyon, their skills and know-how are now combined with modern technologies and this allows the factory to produce lightweight jewellery of very high quality.

Production capacity is for 80,000 pieces a month; a strong design team develops around 1,000 new products every year. Possibility to collaborate with our design team to customize the existing products or even create new products from sketch according to the local markets demands.

The company has a COFRAC accredited laboratory which guarantees our alloys conformity and has recently been certified RJC (membership 00001940). A strict quality control department is in place and this allows that 100% of production is checked before shipment.
